DONATION PROCESS

Donating blood is safe, simple, and rewarding experience that usually only takes 45-60 minutes. Before you donate just make sure you are full filling general donor requirement. find a blood blank near you, make an appointment. when you arrive at the blood donation centre, fill the registration form or you can do this here on the website as well. After that you will be asked genral question in pre-donation screening, in which a drop of your blood will be tested to ensure the iron level is sufficient. After this you will be taken to the donor bed and here you will be donating one unit of blood and it will hardly take 6-10 minutes. following your donation, you will receive refreshments in the canteen area, where you can stay untill you feel strong to leave.
